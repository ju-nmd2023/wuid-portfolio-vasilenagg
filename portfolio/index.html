About me

My name is Vasilena Georgieva. I am 19 years old and I am from Plovdiv, Bulgaria. I studied computer graphics as a high school major in Bulgaria for five years and I have completed two internships in the areas of graphic design and printing. I am currently studying New Media Design in Jönköping University, Sweden.
